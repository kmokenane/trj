How to Use This Trading Calendar

  1. Select your desired month and year using the dropdown menus
  2. Choose your preferred currency from the currency selector
  3. Click "Update Calendar" to generate the calendar view
  4. To add or edit P&L values, click on any day cell
  5. Enter your profit (with + sign) or loss (with - sign) for that day
  6. The calendar will automatically calculate your totals and net P&L
  7. Profits are shown with green backgrounds, losses with red backgrounds
  8. Use the "Export Data" button to save your trading data
  9. Your data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage
